Featured Players

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket, with a record-breaking career spanning over two decades and numerous accolades, including the most runs scored in both Tests and One-Day Internationals (ODIs).

View Profile
Brian Lara

Brian Lara is a West Indian cricket legend and former captain, widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of the sport, holding records for the highest individual score in Test cricket (400 not out) and first-class cricket (501 not out).

View Profile
Shane Warne

Shane Warne, widely considered one of the greatest bowlers in cricket history, was an Australian leg-spinner who took 708 wickets in Test cricket and 293 wickets in One-Day Internationals, mesmerizing batsmen with his mastery of spin and guile.
